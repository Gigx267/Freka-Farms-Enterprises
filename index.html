About Freka Farms

One-Stop Food Supply Chain & Honey Specialists

Freka Farms Enterprises was established to offer a complete food supply chain and logistics solution — from farming, processing, to distribution of agricultural products to both individuals and corporate clients. Prior to official registration with CAC and NAFDAC, the company operated as a family trade for over 10 years, accumulating 12+ years of experience in farming, processing, and distribution. We are 100% active in honey production & distribution and also proud suppliers of premium Arabian Tea.
