Our Heritage

12+ Years of Excellence in Honey & Agriculture

Freka Farms Enterprises was established to offer a one-stop food supply chain and logistics solution — ranging from farming, processing, to distribution of agricultural products to both individuals and corporate clients. Prior to official registration with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) and NAFDAC, the company operated successfully as a family trade for more than 10 years.

The 12 years of hands-on experience in farming, processing, and distribution of agricultural products has shaped Freka Farms into a trusted name. We are 100% active in honey production and distribution, and we also take pride in offering premium Arabian Tea with remarkable wellness properties.

Our Journey

From Family Trade to NAFDAC Approved Brand

🌱 2012 - 2022

Family Trade Era

For over a decade, Freka operated as a family-run agricultural trade, supplying local communities with pure honey, cassava, maize, and other farm products. Built trust through word-of-mouth and quality.

📋 2022

CAC Registration

Official registration with Corporate Affairs Commission as Freka Farms Enterprises, formalizing our operations and expanding our reach.

✅ 2023

NAFDAC Approval

Our honey products received full NAFDAC certification — guaranteeing purity, safety, and compliance with national standards.

🍯 2023 - Present

Expansion & Arabian Tea Launch

Launched premium Arabian Tea alongside our signature honey. Expanded distribution to corporate clients, hotels, and nationwide delivery.

🏆 2024

Recognized Wellness Brand

Thousands of testimonials for managing diabetes, asthma, ulcers. Became a leading name in natural health solutions in Lagos and beyond.

🚀 Future

International Goals

Plans to export pure Nigerian honey and Arabian tea to global markets while maintaining our commitment to quality and healing.
